Our Menu


Masala Dosa

Masala dosa/Masale Dosey is a variation of the popular South Indian dosa, which has its origins in Tuluva Udupi cuisine of Karnataka.


Misal Pav

Misal pav (Marathi: मिसळपाव) is a popular dish from Maharashtra. It consists of misal and pav.


Vada Pav

Vada pav, alternatively spelt wada pao, is a vegetarian fast food dish native to the state of Maharashtra.


Thali

A thali consists of: a grain: rice or flatbread made with wheat, millet, etc. lentils: dal or sambar.


Gulab Jamun

Gulab jamun (also spelled gulaab jamun) lit. "Rose water berry" or "Rose berry" is a sweet confectionary or dessert, originating in the Indian subcontinent and a type of mithai popular in India.
